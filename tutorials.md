# 0:The Proving
Hello Belladonna, you have done well to come this far. This is the final trial to prove yourself worthy to join our coven.

You can walk with the sacred glyphs WASD. The ancient tomes also speak of a "Left Stick" although it is not known to what they are referring.

# 1:The Humble Slume
This pitiful creature is called a slume. I have pacified it with my magic, it will not attack you. You are not a warrior but you must be able to defend yourself if pressed. The spoon you wield will do nicely.

Attack with LMB or, if you are of the kin of XBox or Steam Deck, X or Right Bumper.
Strike it down with your spoon to proceed!

# flee:Give Chase!
This slume cowers before you. Put it out of its misery.

# kill:The First Kill
Our coven protects nature, but these slumes are a corruption of it. They emerge from corrupted quintessence seeping into the natural world.

Proceed to the east Belladonna.

# wandering:A Wandering Slume
This room contains two slumes that have not been pacified. They should be easily slain. However! There is a path to the south containing seeds for your sling. Proceed there now.

# bullets:Bullets
Now that you have ammunition for your sling you can use them to attack. Charge your sling with RMB or Right Trigger and fire with your usual attack button. If you release the charge button you do not expend ammunition.

The sling is less powerful than the spoon but it can bed used at range. Note that you move more slowly when spinning your sling.

# finale:The Grand Finale
To the north of you are a pair of fearsome enemies, much more intimidating than the slumes you have faced thus far. Be wary Belladonna, this is your final test.

# victory:A Witch is You!
Congratulations Belladonna, you have proven yourself capable in the face of adversity. You are finally ready to join our circle.
